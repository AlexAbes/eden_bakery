About Eden Bakery

Hi! Welcome to the Eden Bakery website!

My name is Mika Awolowo and I am a 2015 graduate of the University of Nottingham, having studied Business Management. I am incredibly excited to soon be starting a six-month patisserie course at Le Cordon Bleu in London. For the past couple of years I have invested my time, energy and my sanity into the world of baking. Here I've found passion, beauty and a whole lot of love.

Eden Bakery is the product of a long-held dream of mine; to one day have my own bakery somewhere in the world that pays homage to all the wonderful flavours and textures that baking provides. For now however, all my baking is limited to wherever I am, be that in London or my hometown of Lagos, Nigeria. Eden Bakery is a beginning, with the intention to grow into not only a business but also a movement of spreading love through food.

Visit the gallery to see all I make and my blog as I discuss things I make, how I make them and why I do. If you happen to be in London and you're interested in anything and everything sweet, contact me and I'll make you something in between my lectures and tutorials! Thanks for visiting the website, hope you like it and get involved in my journey!

Lots of Love,
Mika
