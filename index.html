The Dream

At Eden Bakery, we are devoted to creating the perfect confectionery. We knead love into every iota of dough and craft the last word in flawless icing. We aim to bring the dream of perfection in bakery to reality.

Mission

Our simple mission is to make sure that everyone who comes into contact with Eden Bakery will come away feeling cared for, valued and important; baking is truly able to bring this kind of love to everyone's lives.

Bake a difference!

We invite you to join the baking community through Eden Bakery and its founder, Mika Awolowo; bake something for someone you care about and bring joy to a stranger's life.
